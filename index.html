📚 Summary of Core safety principles

  • It is strictly prohibited for metal tools to touch the positive and negative poles
  • Insulating protection (such as glue, sleeves, etc.) must be used.
  • Do not cross over the battery cells during handling.
  • The equipment must be isolated and designed to prevent falling.
  • Storage and transportation must be secured and insulated.
